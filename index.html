⚠ Critical flaw: The mechanism relies entirely on market confidence. If confidence breaks, the seigniorage token crashes — the protocol can't incentivize contractions — and both tokens spiral to zero. This is a death spiral, and it has happened.

Case study · May 2022
The LUNA / TerraUST collapse
$40 billion destroyed in 5 days
Day 1
Large UST withdrawal
$2B withdrawn from Anchor protocol. UST begins to depeg slightly — $0.98.
Day 2
Panic accelerates
UST falls to $0.92. Protocol mints massive amounts of LUNA to restore the peg.
Day 3
LUNA hyperinflation
Billions of LUNA minted. Supply dilution crashes LUNA price. Confidence evaporates.
Day 5
Total collapse
UST → $0.01. LUNA → $0.00. $40B in value destroyed. Peg never recovered.
