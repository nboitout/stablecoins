Stablecoin = Fracturedcoin
Fragmentation is a feature, not a bug — BIS / Hyun Song Shin (2026)
True money requires singleness — one unit accepted seamlessly everywhere. Stablecoins structurally fail this property. Blockchain congestion is deliberately engineered to reward validators, making fragmentation irreducible.
Same issuer, different rails
USDC on Ethereum ≠ USDC on Solana. They are non-fungible tokens referencing the same issuer.
USDT: 107 chains USDC: 125 chains
The bridge tax
Moving between chains adds time, cost, and operational risk. Bridge exploits cost users
$2.5B+
in losses 2021–2024
Congestion is designed in
Capacity is capped to sustain gas fee revenue for validators. A busy chain is a secure chain — high fees are the product, not a problem.
Layer 2 doesn't fix it
L2 lowers costs but creates no shared liquidity pool or settlement layer — it multiplies non-fungible platforms instead.
